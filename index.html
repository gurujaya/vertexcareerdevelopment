At Vertex, we strive to empower international students for success in the U.S. through career development seminars, courses, mentorship, and resources, fostering a supportive community for navigating higher education and global careers. We are committed to democratizing access to career development for international students. Our mission is to provide comprehensive and accessible resources that equip students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to pursue their academic and professional aspirations in the United States!
