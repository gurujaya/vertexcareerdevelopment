Problem

According to the National Association of Colleges and Employers, “International students experience unique career development difficulties.”

Many aspiring students from around the world hope to study and work in America, but are unsure how to start. Despite their aspirations, many encounter difficulties navigating the complex landscape of educational opportunities. 41% of graduate international students worry about their future employment-based visas. Teaching them the necessary skills can enable them to be more confident.

What Vertex Offers

Vertex aims to create high quality educational content regardless of socioeconomic status to provide equal free career development for all. At Vertex, we strive to empower international students for success in the U.S. through: Career Development Webinars, Online Video Course, Mentorship, and Written Resources.

Goals:

  • Expand accessibility to resources and raise awareness of vocational and educational opportunities for international students.
  • Develop international students’ personal finance, saving, and investing proficiency.
  • Understand documentation and the immigration process.
  • Make connections and network with like-minded peers.

We cover the following topics in our online video course:
Our online webinars in partnership with international schools convey the same concepts of these videos, but in an engaging, live setting.

Topics:
Video 1 - 6 minutes (Researching Opportunities)
Topics Covered: Universities, Degrees, Trade Schools, Specific perks of different universities, In-Demand Careers in America, Choosing a career track

Video 2 - 8 minutes (Requirements for Success)
This video was centered around what international students need in order to be successful in college. We dove deep into the requirements for success when it comes to college applications and exploring specific academic and career tracks.
Topics Covered: SATs, standardized test scores, Common App, Grades, Financial Aid, Recommendation Letters, Holistic Application, Pre-Med/Healthcare track overview/specific requirements, Business/Finance track overview/specific requirements, Engineering/CS track overview/specific requirements, Law/Humanities track overview/specific requirements

Video 3 - 5 minutes (Career Advancement)
This video covered the topic of career advancement, providing tips and advice to international students. We discuss the importance of skill development, versatility, internships, resume building, interview preparation, and continuous learning.
Topics Covered: Skill Building, Versatility of Skills, Internships, Resume Development, Interview Advice

Video 4 - 7 minutes (Personal Finance)
Video 4 covered the critical topic of personal finance. Having their personal finances in check is essential for success for all international students. In this video, we covered these key areas: Opening a bank account, saving and investing in the USA, and methods for saving money as an international student. So, let's dive in!
Topics Covered: Basics of Banking and Bank Accounts, Saving and Investing in the US, Financial Markets in the US, Money-Saving Strategies

Video 5 - 7 minutes (Immigration)
Video 5 covered immigration, including important legal documentation and the visa application process. Obtaining a visa is a crucial step for international students, so we drew on our immigration experience and aimed to educate and inform them in an easy-to-understand way.
Topic Covered: F-1 Student Visa, Form I-20 “Certificate of Eligibility for Nonimmigrant Student Status, Paying the I-901 SEVIS Fee, H-1B Visa, DS-160 Visa Application

Video 6 - 7 minutes (Networking and the Workplace)
Video 6 covers the topic of networking and its importance in the workplace. This video emphasizes the ongoing nature of networking and the benefits it brings in terms of career growth and personal development. We provided practical tips to viewers, in order to highlight the significance of networking.
Topics Covered: Making Meaningful Connections, Expanding Your Network both In-Person and Virtually, How To Promote Faster, Maintaining and Nurturing Connections, Seeking Opportunities for Personal Branding
